Safety Tip

Know Before the Thunderstorm Catches You
Today's Special Marine Warning flagged a storm moving at 25 knots near Sand Cut this afternoon. At that speed, a storm can cover open water faster than most boats can reach harbor. If you see building cumulonimbus clouds, hear thunder, or your VHF broadcasts a marine warning, do not wait -- head in immediately. A 30-knot gust on 5-foot seas with a short 5-second period can overwhelm smaller vessels in minutes. This morning's window is your best bet today. If you are out this afternoon, monitor VHF channel 16 continuously.
