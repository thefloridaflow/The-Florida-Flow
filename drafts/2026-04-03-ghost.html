Safety Tip

Thunderstorm on the Water: Get Off Now

A Special Marine Statement is active today for a storm capable of 30-knot gusts near Lake Okeechobee, moving northwest at 25 knots. If you can see a thunderstorm, you are already in range of its lightning. The rule is simple: if you hear thunder, get to shore. Do not wait to see if it passes over you. With seas already at 4.9-5.2 ft offshore and short wave periods, a sudden wind shift from a storm cell will make conditions dangerous very quickly. Port Everglades current is registering near zero at 0.07 kt WSW, which means there is no dominant flow to push the storm away from the coast quickly. Stay off the water until the statement expires and skies are clear.
