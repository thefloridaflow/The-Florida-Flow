🚨 Safety Tip: Cold Front Timing

The most dangerous moment with a cold front is not the day after, when everyone knows it's rough. It is the hours during passage, when wind shifts fast and conditions deteriorate faster than forecast. Today's gusts are predicted at 25 kt, but frontal passages in South Florida can produce brief stronger surges. If you are already on the water this afternoon when the front crosses, head in early. The Gulf Stream in particular responds quickly to post-frontal winds. NWS is forecasting 8-12 ft seas there by Wednesday, which means conditions will be building dangerously by tonight.
