## ⚠️ Safety Tip of the Day

**Waterspouts are possible with this shower.** These rotating vortices can form rapidly and intensify without warning. If you see rotating cloud formation or a funnel over the water, move perpendicular to its path immediately and seek the nearest shelter. Keep a radio tuned to NOAA Weather Radio or VHF Ch. 16 for waterspout warnings.
