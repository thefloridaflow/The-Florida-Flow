🚨 Safety Tip

Plan around the cold front -- not into it.

A cold front is on track to pass Monday with winds building to 18 kt and gusts to 23 kt. The NWS has already flagged the potential for breezy, hazardous conditions. If you are planning any offshore trip this weekend, make sure you can return well before Monday morning -- do not count on conditions holding through Sunday night.

The Gulf Stream wall is currently sitting just 3 nm east of Lake Worth and 7 nm off Port Everglades. During front passage, the interaction between opposing Gulf Stream current and increased wind-driven seas can create steep, confused waves in that zone. Give that area extra clearance on Monday and Tuesday.
