Safety Tip

Waterspouts move fast and form without warning. Today's Special Marine Warning confirmed waterspouts and gusts to nearly 50 knots between Ocean Ridge and Hillsboro Beach, tracking south at 20 knots. If you see a waterspout on the water, do not try to cross its path. Move at 90 degrees to its direction of travel, reduce speed, and get to port immediately. A waterspout can capsize a small vessel within seconds of contact. No dive, no fishing trip, and no photo is worth the risk.
