Safety Tip

Shower & Lightning Protocol
A strong shower with 30-knot gusts and lightning is currently offshore. If you're on the water and see dark clouds or hear thunder, head to shelter immediately—never attempt to outrun a squall line. On land, stay away from tall structures, water, and metal objects. Visibility can drop sharply in heavy rain. Check marine radar before departure and keep VHF handy.
