A brief but intense rain shower with winds gusting to 30 knots is moving through Jupiter Inlet to Ocean Reef waters. The NWS warns that waterspouts are possible with this activity. If you are on the water in that zone, monitor the sky for rotating cloud formations over water, stay alert to VHF radio, and move immediately away from any observed rotation.
