Waterspout Awareness During Offshore Showers
A strong rain shower capable of producing winds near 30 knots is currently moving west across Atlantic waters east of Broward County, with waterspout activity possible. If you are on the water offshore, monitor the system closely: watch for rapid wind shifts, a darkening sky, and rotating cloud formations. If a waterspout is spotted or a waterspout warning is issued, move perpendicular to its track, reduce speed, and seek shelter in protected waters or marina immediately. Nearshore and bay conditions are unaffected. Verify with your captain before departing for offshore destinations.
