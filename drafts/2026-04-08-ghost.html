Safety Tip

Gusts to 39 kt: Know the difference between wind speed and gust speed.

Sustained winds tonight are forecast at 24 kt, but gusts are reaching 39 kt. That 15-knot gap matters enormously on a small vessel or paddleboard. A gust can push you off course, swamp an open boat, or knock a paddler into the water before they have time to brace. Do not plan your trip around the sustained wind number alone.

The cancelled Special Marine Warning from NWS earlier today is a reminder that conditions can change faster than forecasts update. Monitor VHF weather radio channel WX1 before and during any on-water activity. If you hear thunder, get off the water immediately.
