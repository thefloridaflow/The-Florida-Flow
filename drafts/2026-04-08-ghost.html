Safety Tip

Gusts vs. sustained wind: know the difference before you launch.
Tonight's forecast shows sustained winds of 24 kt with gusts reaching 39 kt off Palm Beach. That 15 kt gap between sustained and gusts is significant. A boat or kayak that handles 24 kt winds can be overwhelmed by a sudden 39 kt gust, especially in beam-sea conditions. Always plan your trip around the gust speed, not the sustained figure. If gusts are more than 10 kt above sustained, conditions are unstable and the sea state will be irregular. When in doubt, stay in.
