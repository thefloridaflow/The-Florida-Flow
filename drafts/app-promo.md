# Check Conditions Before You Go. The Dashboard Is Live.

*The Florida Flow*

---

You're already getting the daily newsletter. Now there's a place to check conditions any time — not just at 5am when the email lands.

**[The Florida Flow dashboard](https://the-florida-flow.vercel.app)** pulls everything together in one page, updated every hour:

**Live NOAA buoy data** across 6 stations — Cape Canaveral to the Keys. Wave height, period, water temp, wind. The numbers, not a vibe.

**Tides + BHB Dive Windows** — the full high/low tide table for today and tomorrow, with Blue Heron Bridge entry/exit windows and quality ratings (Optimal / Good / Fair) built right in. No more cross-referencing two tabs.

**NWS Marine Forecast** — the actual Zone AMZ630 forecast text, not a watered-down summary.

**Operator Logs** — latest conditions reports from Narcosis Dive (West Palm Beach) and Rainbow Reef (Key Largo): visibility, current, water temp, and captain notes pulled fresh from their sites.

**UV index, Port Everglades tidal current, sun times** — everything you need to plan the session start to finish.
