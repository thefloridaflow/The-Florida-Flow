Safety Tip

Thunderstorms Move Fast. So Should You. Today's NWS statement confirmed a strong thunderstorm near Sand Cut moving at 25 knots capable of 30-knot wind gusts. At that speed, a storm can cover 5 nautical miles in about 12 minutes. If you see lightning or a dark anvil cloud on the horizon, do not wait to see how close it gets. Head for shore immediately and stay off the water until at least 30 minutes after the last lightning strike.
