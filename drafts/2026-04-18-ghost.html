Safety Tip

Plan around the front, not into it. Today and Sunday offer the best conditions of the week before Monday's cold front pushes wind to 18 kt with gusts to 23 kt and significant rain. If you are planning a weekend offshore trip, go today or push to Tuesday afternoon at the earliest as conditions are still easing post-front. The NWS explicitly flags potential hazardous conditions around the Tuesday front passage, so do not assume Tuesday is safe without checking updated forecasts. Always file a float plan before leaving the dock and carry a VHF radio.
