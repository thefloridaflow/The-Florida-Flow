Pre-Front Window: Plan Your Exit Before Monday

A cold front is expected to bring 18kt sustained winds and 23kt gusts by Monday, along with rain and rough seas. If you are planning an offshore trip this weekend, build in a full day of buffer before Monday. Conditions can deteriorate faster than forecast models predict as a front approaches.
