⚠️ Safety Tip

Plan around the cold front, not after it arrives.

Monday's cold front is forecast to bring gusts up to 23 kt and a 42% chance of rain with 6.3 mm of accumulation. NWS has flagged the potential for breezy, hazardous conditions on both Monday and Tuesday. If you have a trip planned for early next week, consider moving it to Saturday or Sunday.

Fronts in South Florida can accelerate faster than models predict. Do not wait for conditions to deteriorate before making the call to stay in. File a float plan, monitor VHF Channel 16, and always tell someone where you are going.
