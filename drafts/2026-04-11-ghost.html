Safety Tip

Waterspouts move fast and form without warning. Today's Special Marine Warning confirmed waterspouts near Hillsboro Beach moving south at 20 knots. A waterspout can travel over a mile in three minutes. If you see a funnel or rotating spray on the water, do not attempt to outrun it on a heading parallel to its path. Turn perpendicular to its direction of travel and move away immediately. Never assume a waterspout will dissipate before it reaches you.
